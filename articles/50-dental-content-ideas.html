50 Dental Content Ideas for Social Media (Never Run Out of Posts)

By Dental Marketing Society March 5, 2026 10 min read

One of the biggest challenges dental practices face with social media is consistent content creation. You know you should be posting 3-5 times per week, but what do you post about? Before you know it, weeks go by without activity, your followers drop off, and you abandon social media entirely.

This guide provides 50 proven content ideas specifically for dental practices. Mix and match these concepts to build a month of content in advance, then repurpose and rotate them throughout the year. You'll never run out of ideas again.

Before & After Results (Post these 2-3x per week)

  1. Cosmetic smile makeover before/after with detailed explanation
  2. Teeth whitening results with timeline and cost information
  3. Invisalign straightening progress photos from patient journey
  4. Dental implant transformation showing natural-looking results
  5. Composite bonding enhancement for chipped teeth
  6. Crown or veneer placement results
  7. Smile design transformation after gum recontouring
  8. Teeth color change after cosmetic bonding
  9. Orthodontic before/after showing full treatment results
  10. Emergency repair transformation (broken tooth fixed)

Educational Oral Health Tips (Post 2-3x per week)

  1. Proper brushing technique video (90 seconds)
  2. How to floss correctly (with common mistakes)
  3. Foods that stain your teeth (and foods that whiten)
  4. How to prevent gum disease (3-step process infographic)
  5. Cavity prevention tips for families with kids
  6. Tobacco and dental health: what you need to know
  7. Alcohol's effect on dental health
  8. Sports drink warning: acidic drinks erode teeth
  9. How to choose the right toothbrush (electric vs. manual)
  10. Mouthwash myths: what actually works
  11. Why you need regular cleanings (break down what happens)
  12. Oral cancer screening facts and risk factors
  13. Dry mouth: causes and solutions
  14. Bad breath causes and remedies
  15. Teeth grinding damage and prevention strategies

Team & Practice Culture (Post 1-2x per week)

  1. Meet the team: dentist bio with fun personal facts
  2. Meet the team: hygienist spotlight
  3. Meet the team: office manager feature
  4. Day-in-the-life behind-the-scenes vlog
  5. Office tour video showing new equipment/renovations
  6. Team celebration: work anniversary spotlight
  7. Staff training moment: team learning new technology
  8. Community volunteer moment: team helping local cause
  9. Team fun: office party or celebration photos
  10. Dentist going the extra mile: heartwarming patient story

Patient Testimonials & Success Stories (Post 1-2x per week)

  1. Video testimonial: patient discusses their smile makeover journey
  2. Quote graphic: patient testimonial as image post
  3. Success story: long-time patient shares their history with practice
  4. Parent testimonial: how practice made their child comfortable
  5. Fear-to-confidence story: anxious patient overcomes dental anxiety
  6. Transformation story: patient regains confidence in their smile

Seasonal & Holiday Content (Post 1x per month, adjusted seasonally)

  1. New Year, New Smile: dental resolution inspiration post
  2. Valentine's Day: smile confidently for date night tips
  3. Spring cleaning tips including dental health refresh
  4. Back-to-school special announcement and orthodontics info
  5. Summer whitening special and smile-before-vacation tips
  6. Halloween candy warning: how to protect teeth from Halloween candy
  7. Thanksgiving gratitude: thankful for your patients post
  8. Holiday smile tips: how to avoid holiday food stains
  9. New Year promotion announcement

Educational Procedure Guides (Post 1-2x per month)

  1. Root canal explained in 60 seconds (bust the myth!)
  2. What to expect during your first appointment
  3. Crown procedure walkthrough with timeline
  4. Dental implant process from start to finish
  5. Scaling and root planing: what gum disease patients need to know
  6. Emergency dentistry: what counts as a dental emergency
  7. Teeth whitening options compared (professional vs. at-home)
  8. Orthodontic options: braces vs. Invisalign vs. clear aligners
  9. Gum grafting procedure: when and why
  10. Extraction and bone graft procedure explained

That's 60 content ideas! Here's how to use them strategically:

Content Calendar Strategy

Weekly mix:

  • Monday: Educational tip (reaches Monday morning scrollers)
  • Wednesday: Before/after result (mid-week inspiration)
  • Friday: Team culture or patient testimonial (weekend planning/family chatting)
  • Bonus posts: Stories 2-3x per week with behind-the-scenes content

Create batches: Instead of creating individual posts, batch create content in batches. One day, shoot 4-6 videos of your dentist explaining procedures. Another day, photograph 8-10 before/afters. Batch creation is 3x faster than creating one post at a time.

Repurpose and rotate: A before/after can be posted on Instagram, then Facebook, then LinkedIn. A testimonial video can become a Reel, then a Story, then a post caption. You can reuse content every 3-4 months since not everyone sees every post.

Plan ahead: Use these 50 ideas to plan 50 posts (almost a year of weekly posting). You never have to scramble for content again.

Content Format Variations

Each content idea can be formatted multiple ways for different platforms:

  • Image post: Static image with text caption
  • Carousel post: Multiple images (before/afters are perfect for carousels)
  • Reel/Short video: 15-60 second video version
  • Story: Quick 15-second teaser version
  • IGTV/YouTube: Extended 3-5 minute educational version
  • Blog post: Detailed 1,000+ word version of educational content

One content piece can become 6 different social media posts across different formats and platforms.

Engagement Strategies

These 50 content ideas are only effective if you engage with your audience:

  • Ask questions in captions ("What's your biggest smile concern?")
  • Respond to every comment within the first 2 hours
  • Ask followers to share their own before/afters (user-generated content)
  • Run monthly polls in Stories to understand patient interests
  • Create shareable educational content (infographics, quick tips)
  • Feature patient-submitted questions and answer them in videos

Analytics & Optimization

Track which content types perform best:

  • Do before/afters get more engagement than educational posts?
  • Are videos or images more engaging?
  • What topics get the most questions/messages?
  • What posting time gets the most engagement?

Double down on what works. If before/afters consistently get 2x the engagement of other content, post them more frequently.

Next Steps

Pick 10 of these 50 ideas that resonate most with your practice. Batch create content for those 10 ideas (photo/video 4-6 variations of each). Upload to your social media scheduler for the next month. Then repeat. In 3-4 months, you'll have a library of 40-50 pieces of content ready to rotate year-round.

This is the fastest path to consistent social media that builds your brand and attracts new patients without the daily stress of creating content from scratch.
