Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Foundation tier?
+
The Foundation tier includes 25+ expert articles, the Marketing Scorecard tool, monthly newsletter, read-only community access, and a basic learning path. It's perfect for practice owners just getting started with dental marketing and it's completely free—forever.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
+
Yes! You can upgrade from Foundation to Growth or Elite anytime, and you can downgrade to a lower tier whenever you like. There are no long-term contracts or commitments. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
How does the free trial work?
+
All new Growth and Elite members get 2 months completely free. After the trial period ends, your membership will automatically continue at the monthly price ($29 for Growth, $79 for Elite). You can cancel anytime during or after your trial—no credit card required to start.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and ACH bank transfers for annual subscriptions. All payments are secure and processed through industry-standard payment gateways.
Is there an annual discount?
+
Yes! Pay annually and save 28%: Growth is $249/year (normally $29/month = $348/year), and Elite is $699/year (normally $79/month = $948/year). You still get the 2-month free trial, and you can cancel anytime with a pro-rated refund.
Can my team members join?
+
Foundation and Growth tiers are for individual members. Elite members can share their login with up to 2 additional team members. For larger teams or multi-location practices, please contact us about custom group pricing—we offer special rates for DSOs and group practices.
What happens if I cancel my membership?
+
You can cancel anytime with no penalties. Your access continues through the end of your current billing period. If you've paid for an annual plan and cancel early, we'll provide a pro-rated refund for unused months. Your data and learning progress is saved if you decide to rejoin later.
Do you offer group or DSO pricing?
+
Absolutely! We offer custom volume pricing for dental groups, multi-location practices, and DSOs. Contact our team to discuss your specific needs and get a personalized quote. Most groups save 35-50% off regular per-seat pricing.
