Membership FAQs

Can I switch membership levels anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your membership at any time. Your billing will be prorated, so you won't overpay or lose money. Changes take effect immediately.

Is the Basic tier really unlimited webinars?

Absolutely. Basic members get unlimited access to all live webinars, recordings, and the monthly webinar library. We host 4-8 live webinars per month across different topics.

What's included in the Annual Summit ticket?

Premium members get a full 3-day virtual ticket to our October Annual Summit (value $297). This includes all 30+ expert sessions, networking events, and CE credits. You can also add a hotel package for the in-person option.

Do Premium advisory sessions come with Ekwa consulting?

No. The 2 annual advisory sessions are with DMS experts and can cover any aspect of your marketing strategy. You can also add paid consulting from Ekwa Marketing if you want, but it's completely optional.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly memberships can be cancelled anytime with no penalties. Annual memberships have a 30-day cancellation window for a full refund, or you can downgrade to a lower tier at any time.

Do I get CE credits with Basic membership?

Yes! Basic members get free CE credits for all live webinars. Most webinars offer 1-2 CE credits. Premium members also get credits for all events including masterminds and workshops.

What about team members? Can multiple people from my practice join?

With Explorer and Basic, one person per membership. Premium allows you to add up to 2 team members. Enterprise allows unlimited team members at all locations. Contact us for team pricing.

Is there a free trial for Basic or Premium?

No formal trial, but you can start with Explorer free and get a real feel for the community and content. Basic is just $49/month, and you can cancel anytime if it's not right for you.

What if I'm a corporate DSO or health system?

Reach out to our team at contact@dentalsociety.com. We have enterprise pricing for DSOs, health systems, and corporate groups with 10+ locations.

Do you offer payment plans?

We offer monthly and annual billing for all tiers. Annual billing saves 5-10% vs. monthly. Contact us for custom payment arrangements if needed.
