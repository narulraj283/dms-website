What You'll Learn

Your online reputation directly impacts new patient acquisition. Patients research your practice on Google, Facebook, and review platforms before booking. In this channel, you'll learn how to systematically generate more reviews from satisfied patients, respond professionally to all feedback, handle negative reviews strategically, continuously monitor your online reputation, leverage patient testimonials for marketing, and excel on all major review platforms.

Systematically ask satisfied patients to leave reviews. Timing, messaging, and processes that work without being pushy.

💬 Review Responses

⚠️ Negative Reviews

Deal with negative feedback professionally. Turning critics into advocates. Legal and ethical considerations.

📊 Reputation Monitoring

Stay on top of what patients say. Monitor Google, Facebook, Yelp, and specialty dental review platforms in real time.

Collect and showcase video and written testimonials. Use social proof in marketing. Getting permission and releasing testimonials.
