Workflow 2: Patient Reactivation Campaign

Multi-touch reactivation sequence triggered for patients with 180+ days since last visit

🎯 Trigger

Patient with 180+ days since last visit

Email 1: "We Miss You"

Emotional re-engagement message highlighting what they've missed and new services available

⏳ Wait 7 Days

Monitor for opens, clicks, or appointment bookings

Email 2: "Special Reactivation Offer"

Present limited-time offer to incentivize appointment booking (e.g., 20% off cleaning)

⏳ Wait 14 Days

Second monitoring period for engagement

Email 3: "Last Chance" (Final Email)

Urgency-driven final appeal emphasizing appointment availability and practice value

📞 No Response Action

Create phone call task in CRM to reach out personally
