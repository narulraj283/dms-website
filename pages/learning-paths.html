Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need membership to access the learning paths?
Learning paths are included with Basic and Premium DMS memberships, both currently free. If you're on the free Explorer plan, upgrade to Basic for full access to all learning paths and certifications.
How long does it take to complete a learning path?
Foundation Path takes approximately 4-6 weeks at a typical pace of 5-8 hours per week. Growth Path takes 8-10 weeks, and Mastery Path takes 6-8 weeks. You can progress at your own pace, with no time limits.
What happens if I don't pass the certification exam?
You can retake the exam as many times as you need. We recommend reviewing the module materials and using the practice quizzes before your next attempt. Most members pass on the second try.
Can I skip modules or start in the middle of a path?
We recommend completing modules in order for the best learning experience, but you can skip ahead if you already have expertise in certain areas. However, assessment scores may vary if you haven't covered prerequisite content.
Are the certifications recognized outside of DMS?
DMS certifications are recognized within the dental marketing industry and demonstrate expertise to patients, vendors, and peers. We're working on state board CE credit approval. Your certificates are verifiable on LinkedIn and can be displayed on your website.
