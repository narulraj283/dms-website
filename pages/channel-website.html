What You'll Learn

Your website is your digital front desk. It's where prospects land from Google, ads, and social media. A poorly designed site loses you patients. In this channel, you'll learn how to design websites that look professional, structure pages for conversion, optimize patient experience on mobile and desktop, enable frictionless online scheduling, ensure fast page speeds, and use A/B testing to continuously improve results.

🌐 Website Design

Professional dental website design. Colors, typography, layout, imagery, and brand consistency. Design principles that work.

👥 Patient Experience

Make your site easy to use. Navigation, information hierarchy, and reducing friction. What patients actually want to see.

🎯 Conversion Optimization

Turn visitors into leads. Call-to-actions, forms, landing page structure, and urgency tactics that work ethically.

📅 Online Scheduling

Implement online booking that patients love. Integration, user experience, and reducing no-shows.

⚡ Mobile & Performance

Mobile-first design and page speed. 60% of traffic is mobile. Every second counts for conversions.

🧪 A/B Testing

Test everything. Headlines, CTAs, forms, and colors. Data-driven decisions improve results continuously.
