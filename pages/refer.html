Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my unique referral link?

Log into your DMS member dashboard and navigate to the Referral Program section. Your unique referral link is displayed prominently. Copy it and share it wherever you'd like — email, LinkedIn, Slack, social media, or in person. Every referral using your link will be automatically tracked.

When do I get my rewards?

Your referral counts immediately once your colleague signs up using your link. Once they activate their membership (usually within 24 hours), your reward gets applied to your account. For example, if you're at 6 referrals, you'll have 6 months of free Elite access added to your account. You can track everything in real-time on your dashboard.

Can I share my referral link on social media or my website?

Absolutely! Share your referral link anywhere. You can add it to your email signature, website footer, LinkedIn bio, Facebook, Instagram, or anywhere else you'd like. The more places you share it, the more referrals you'll typically get. Just make sure each share feels authentic and relevant to your audience.

Do my referrals stay active forever or expire?

Your referrals count permanently. Once someone signs up using your link and activates their membership, that referral is locked in and counts toward your tier forever. This means your rewards keep compounding. Even if a referred member leaves DMS later, the referral still counts toward your lifetime total and tier benefits.

What happens if I reach Ambassador status (11+ referrals)?

Congratulations! You're now a DMS Ambassador. You get lifetime Elite access, an exclusive Ambassador badge for your profile, direct access to the leadership team, invitation to exclusive roundtables, speaking opportunities at DMS events, and priority support. You also join a community of the top 5% of referrers who are shaping the future of DMS.
