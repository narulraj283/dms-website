Direct Mail Marketing

Reach new patients in your area with tangible mail. Create compelling postcards, target new movers and high-value neighborhoods, track ROI precisely, and integrate with digital for maximum impact.

Access Full Channel Upcoming Webinars →

What You'll Learn

Direct mail is a powerful way to reach high-intent patients in your geographic area. With less competition than digital channels, postcards get opened and noticed. In this channel, you'll learn how to design postcards that convert, use EDDM to target specific neighborhoods, execute new mover campaigns, calculate and track ROI accurately, create seasonal campaigns, and integrate direct mail with digital marketing for maximum results.

📬 Postcard Design

Create postcards that get noticed. Design principles, copywriting, offers, and examples that work.

🏘️ New Mover Targeting

Find people moving to your area. Data sources, timing, and campaigns that win new patients from movers.

📍 EDDM Campaigns

Every Door Direct Mail. Target by zip, neighborhood, and demographics. Best practices for dental practices.

📊 ROI & Tracking

Know your numbers. Tracking unique codes, phone numbers, and attributing patients to campaigns.

🎉 Seasonal Campaigns

Timing matters. Holiday campaigns, New Year promotions, and seasonal messaging that drives bookings.

🔗 Digital Integration

Combine mail with email, SMS, and landing pages. Multi-channel strategies that maximize response.
