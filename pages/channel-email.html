Build a loyal patient base with strategic email. Create newsletters, patient reactivation campaigns, appointment reminders, and automated sequences that keep patients engaged and coming back.

What You'll Learn

Email is the highest-ROI marketing channel for dental practices. Unlike social media where algorithms decide your reach, email gives you direct access to patients' inboxes. In this channel, you'll learn how to build an engaged email list, create newsletters patients actually read, automate appointment reminders that reduce no-shows, reactivate past patients, and segment your list for personalized campaigns that drive bookings and referrals.

✉️ Email Fundamentals

List building strategies, email compliance, deliverability, and setting up your email platform for dental practices.

📰 Newsletter Design

Create newsletters that get opened and clicked. Subject lines, content strategy, templates, and design best practices.

🎯 Patient Segmentation

Divide your list by patient type, treatment status, and engagement. Send the right message to the right person.

🔄 Reactivation Campaigns

Win back dormant patients. Campaign sequences, timing, messaging, and offers that work for dental practices.

⏰ Appointment Automation

Automated reminders, confirmations, and post-appointment follow-ups. Reduce no-shows and get reviews.

📊 Email Analytics

Measure open rates, click rates, conversions, and ROI. Improve your campaigns with data.
