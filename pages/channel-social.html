What You'll Learn

Social media is where patients spend their time every day. It's the perfect place to build brand awareness, showcase your practice, engage with patients, and attract new ones. In this channel, you'll develop a platform-specific strategy for Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok, create content that your followers love, engage authentically with your community, plan dental-specific content ideas, run profitable paid social campaigns, and measure success with actionable analytics.
