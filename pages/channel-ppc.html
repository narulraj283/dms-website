What You'll Learn

PPC advertising puts your practice in front of patients who are actively searching for dental services right now. Unlike organic tactics that take months, PPC can drive qualified leads within days. In this channel, you'll master Google Ads for high-intent keywords, learn Facebook and Instagram advertising to build awareness, target the right dental patients, write ad copy that converts, build high-performing landing pages, and measure every dollar spent to maximize ROI.
